About Us

At Netcarbon, we are dedicated to monitoring and supporting nature-based carbon offsets. Our clientele encompasses stakeholders in the Voluntary Carbon Market, including Environmental Agencies, Think Tanks, Universities, and Intergovernmental Organizations such as the UN and World Bank.

Our innovative dashboard utilizes satellite imagery and AI tools to monitor changes in land use and deforestation rates within the DRC. This powerful tool aids our team and clients in making informed decisions, tracking project impacts, and implementing effective conservation strategies.
